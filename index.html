Step-by-Step Campaign Setup Guide

How to launch these creatives in Meta Ads Manager

Step 1 — Create a New Campaign

  1. Go to Meta Ads Manager → Click + Create
  2. Choose your campaign objective based on funnel stage (see table below)
  3. Name your campaign: TUM — [Funnel Stage] — [Month Year]
  4. Enable Advantage Campaign Budget (CBO) if running multiple ad sets
Ad #Funnel StageRecommended Objective
Ad 1 — Hero/AuthorityColdAwareness or Engagement
Ad 2 — LifestyleColdAwareness or Traffic
Ad 3 — Fat LossProblem-AwareTraffic or Leads
Ad 4 — PerformanceBenefit-DrivenTraffic or Leads
Ad 5 — MindsetIdentity-BasedEngagement or Traffic
Ad 6 — Program StructureTrust BuilderLeads or Traffic
Ad 7 — UrgencyConversionLeads or Conversions
Ad 8 — DM CTABottom-FunnelMessages (Instagram Direct)

Step 2 — Set Up Ad Sets

  1. Set your daily or lifetime budget
  2. Choose placements: Manual Placements → select Facebook Feed + Instagram Feed + Instagram Explore
  3. Set audience targeting (see audience section below)
  4. Schedule: start immediately or set a launch date

Step 3 — Create the Ads

  1. Select Single Image format
  2. Upload the saved PNG creative (use the Save Image buttons above)
  3. Paste the Primary Text into the primary text field
  4. Paste the Headline into the headline field
  5. Paste the Link Description into the description field
  6. Select the matching CTA Button from the dropdown
  7. Enter your destination URL with UTM parameters (see below)
  8. Preview across all placements and publish

Audience Targeting Suggestions per Funnel Stage

Recommended targeting for each creative type

Cold Audiences (Ads 1 & 2)

  • Broad interest targeting: Kickboxing, MMA, Boxing, GLORY Kickboxing, UFC, Martial Arts
  • Fitness interests: Fitness & Wellness, Weight Training, Bodybuilding, CrossFit, Athletic Performance
  • Lifestyle interests: Men's Health, GQ, Self-improvement, Entrepreneurship, Personal Development
  • Demographics: Men, Age 22–45
  • Locations: Netherlands, Belgium, UK, Germany (expand based on program delivery — online = worldwide)
  • Lookalike audiences: 1–3% LAL based on Instagram engagers, website visitors, or customer list

Problem-Aware & Benefit-Driven (Ads 3 & 4)

  • Retargeting: Video viewers (25%, 50%, 75%), Instagram/Facebook engagers (90 days)
  • Stacked interests: Fat Loss + Gym + Meal Prep OR Athletic Performance + Sports Training + Coaching
  • Lookalike: 1–2% LAL of website visitors or Instagram profile visitors
  • Exclude: Existing customers / applicants

Identity-Based & Trust Builder (Ads 5 & 6)

  • Retargeting: Website visitors (30 days), Instagram engagers (60 days), Video viewers (50%+)
  • Layered interests: Discipline + Personal Development + High-Performance Coaching
  • Custom audiences: Email list segments (warm leads, newsletter subscribers)

Conversion & Bottom-Funnel (Ads 7 & 8)

  • Hot retargeting: Website visitors (7–14 days), Application page visitors who didn't complete, Cart/checkout abandoners
  • Engaged audiences: People who DMed the Instagram account, Saved posts, Clicked previous ads
  • Custom audiences: Email list of warm leads, People who watched 75%+ of video content
  • Exclude: Already-enrolled clients
